    How to Book
    Contact the Group Sales Office at 202-807-3232 or groupsales@ngs.org Hours: Monday to Friday 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. EST

    OR fill out an online reservation form and email it to groupsales@ngs.org. Please allow up to 48 business hours for a reply.

    All reservation requests are subject to availability. Receipt of payment and completed letter of agreement guarantees your reservation. Processing fees will apply to group rates.
    Accessibility
    Please call two weeks prior to your visit for sign language interpretation, captioning, or hearing assistance devices.

    The ramp to access the museum is located on M Street just east of 17th Street.

    A limited number of complimentary wheelchairs are available on a first-come, first-served basis.
    Visiting Information
    Detailed visit information, including arrival process and bus drop-off/pick-up information will be sent to you as part of your group reservation confirmation.

    All exhibitions at the National Geographic Museum are designed to be self-guided. See FAQ for information about reserving a guided tour. Visitor service staff and volunteer docents are often available to answer questions.

    Cancellation and Refund Policy
    No refunds will be made for cancellations made fewer than 30 days prior to your visit date. Reservations canceled more than 30 days in advance of your visit date are refundable in full. Please notify the Group Sales Office at 202-857-7700 of any changes to your reservation as soon as possible. Should you need to reschedule your visit or make changes to the number of tickets in your reservation, we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

    Inclement Weather
    National Geographic does not generally close due to weather conditions. Call 202-857-7700 to inquire whether the facility is open or closed. If the recording does not mention a closing, National Geographic is open. When the facility is open, tickets are nonrefundable. If the facility closes due to inclement weather, group reservations may be rescheduled by contacting the Group Sales Office. Individual ticket holders will be offered alternate viewing hours, which will be posted to National Geographic’s website and phone line as soon as possible.
    FAQs
    Do you have bus parking?
    We do not have dedicated bus parking. However, you are welcome to pull in front of our main entrance located at 1145 17th Street NW just south of M Street to unload and load your bus.

    Where can I park a bus?
    The District Department of Transportation has a wonderful website that can help guide you through your trip to the museum, including parking. Learn more on their website at http://ddot.dc.gov/service/tour-bus-parking; they can also be reached by phone at 202.671.0682. There is paid parking directly across the street for personal vehicles and the closest dedicated bus parking is on the southbound side of 15th Street between E Street NW and Constitution Avenue NW.

    Can I schedule a 3-D film screening for my group?
    Yes, we can schedule screenings of 3-D films for groups of 25 people or more. The fee is $5 per person plus a $50 reservation fee and based on availability. We typically require two weeks advance notice to schedule a screening.

    How far in advance do I need to make a reservation?
    Ideally, we would like to have two weeks notice before your group’s arrival. We understand that attendance may change and we encourage you to make reservations with a conservative guest count. We can make adjustments closer to your visit, if necessary.

    Is there space for my students to have lunch?
    The National Geographic Museum does not have space for groups to have lunch.

    What’s the largest group I can bring?
    The museum can accommodate groups as large as 150 and can typically keep your visit under two hours. We often have the group split into smaller, more manageable numbers to make your time here more enjoyable. If you have an exceptionally large group it may take longer to get everyone through the exhibitions.

    Can my group reserve a guided tour?
    Yes, guided tours are $50 per tour and take approximately 45 minutes. We can accommodate up to 25 people per tour. If you have a larger group we can do multiple tours, each tour costing $50. This can increase the amount of time it takes to visit the museum.

    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

    How can I purchase an Annual Pass?
    You can purchase an Annual Pass online, over the phone with our ticket office, or in person at the museum ticket desk. We recommend purchasing your Annual Pass in person so that a museum staff member can provide you with your welcome packet and all information about your membership.
    How can I give an Annual Pass as a gift?
    To give an Annual Pass as a gift, please purchase a gift certificate for the price of the Annual Pass you would like to gift. We ask that you do not purchase an Annual Pass for someone else.
    How do I reserve tickets with my Annual Pass?
    To reserve tickets for the National Geographic Museum, you can either call the ticket office at 202-857-7700 or visit the museum ticket desk. Unfortunately, we do not have a way for Annual Pass holders to reserve tickets online at this time.

    To reserve tickets for Nat Geo Live and Nat Geo Nights events, you can purchase online, over the phone, or in person. Please select the Annual Pass holder price type when purchasing online.
    Will I be able to get into the museum with my Annual Pass if I don't reserve ahead of time?
    Annual Pass holders can always get tickets to the museum, even on weekends and holidays. On days we expect to be busy, we hold a number of tickets per hour for our Annual Pass holders.
    I bought an Annual Pass. When can I expect to receive my magazine?
    Annual Pass holders can expect to receive their first magazine 4-6 weeks after the date of purchase. If you do not receive a magazine after 8 weeks, please e-mail our ticket office at ngtickets@ngs.org or inform a museum staff member upon your next visit.
    When does my Annual Pass expire?
    Annual Passes expire 12 months from the date of purchase at the end of the month. For example, an Annual Pass purchased on June 14, 2017 would expire on June 30, 2018. To check if your Annual Pass is still active, please call our ticket office at 202-857-7700 or e-mail us at ngtickets@ngs.org.
    How can I renew my Annual Pass?
    To renew your Annual Pass, please call our ticket office at 202-857-7700 or visit our museum ticket desk.
